Where to point your buys

This isn't rotation. You never sell your winners.

The VIX doesn't just tell you when to buy — it tells you what to buy next. You stay invested the whole way through and let winners run; you just aim each new dollar where the fear level says the reward is. In extreme panic, high-beta tech snaps back hardest. In dead calm, fresh money goes to resilient, cash-generative names — because that's when a surprise is most likely to arrive.

The loop: calm → accumulate quality + build cash → spike → deploy dry powder into high-beta → hold through the recovery. Same index, opposite target at each end — no selling required.
