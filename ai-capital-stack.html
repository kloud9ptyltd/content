Ranked by total economic exposure

Direct revenue understates the entanglement. Companies are ranked on how much of their economic future is wired to OpenAI + Anthropic across every channel. Two ideas drive the order: relative exposure beats absolute (Oracle and CoreWeave outrank Microsoft because one deal is a multiple of their business, while for Microsoft it is a slice of a giant), and derivative / reflexive exposure counts (memory, optics and power makers never sign with the labs, yet their demand is gated by the labs' buildout — and Nvidia funds the very customers who then buy its chips).
